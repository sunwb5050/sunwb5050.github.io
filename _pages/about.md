Aboud me
======
Dr. Wenbo Sun is a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering, the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. His research interests focus on control and optimization in transportation systems, particularly involving drones and connected and autonomous vehicles. Dr. Sun received his Ph.D. degree from the University of Hong Kong, supervised by [Prof. Fangni Zhang](https://www.dase.hku.hk/people/f-n-zhang). Prior to coming to the University of Hong Kong, Dr. Sun had a one-year online Ph.D. journey at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, the University of Minnesota, supervised by [Prof. Zongxuan Sun](https://cse.umn.edu/me/zongxuan-sun). Dr. Sun received his bachelor degree and master degree in Automotive Engineering from Beihang University, advised by [Prof. Xiangyang Xu](https://shi.buaa.edu.cn/xuxiangyang/zh_CN/index.htm), in 2017 and 2020, respectively.

Research interests
======
* Low-altitude logistics

* Multi-modal route optimization problems (e.g., truck-drone, crowdshipping)

* Connected and autonomous vehicles (e.g., traffic prediction, vehicle control)

* Operational research and reinforcement learning
