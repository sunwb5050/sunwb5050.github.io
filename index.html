About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Fangni Zhang. My current research is about trajectory control for autonomous vehicles and route optimization for truck-and-drone collaboration systems.
Prior to coming to the University of Hong Kong, I had a one-year online Ph.D. journey at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, the University of Minnesota, advised by Prof. Zongxuan Sun. I received my bachelor degree and master degree in Automotive Engineering from Beihang University, advised by Prof. Xiangyang Xu, in 2017 and 2020, respectively.
Any suggestions and comments on my website are warmly welcomed!
